A PORTABLE TRACKING AND SAFETY DEVICE FOR THE STUDENTS OF LUCSUHIN INTEGRATED SCHOOL

2024 - 2025

This project enhances student safety at Lucsuhin Integrated School, a rural area with limited security measures. The Student Safety and Tracking System provides real-time GPS tracking, video monitoring, and an emergency alert feature to improve security and response times.

Key Features:

  • ✔ Real-Time Location Tracking – Uses GPS and an Android app to keep parents informed of their child's location.
  • ✔ Video Surveillance – Integrates an ESP32-CAM for live video monitoring.
  • ✔ Emergency Alert System – A multi-functional button allows students to send instant distress signals to parents.
  • ✔ Secure Android App – Ensures seamless communication between the device and users while prioritizing data security.
